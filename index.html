Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27.12.2025

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application "Concrete Calculator" (hereinafter referred to as the "Application").

1. Data Collection

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information. All calculations and data entered by the user (such as dimensions, volumes, and calculation history) are stored exclusively locally on the user's device using the device's local storage.

2. Permissions

The Application does not require any special permissions that would grant access to user data or device functions.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking frameworks.

4. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the contact information provided on the application's listing page in the Google Play Store.
